Timing Guide

Lift days: pre-workout + 5g creatine 45 min before training. Protein shake within 30 min after.

Trip days: eat every 3–4 hours. Pack meals + jerky/protein bar backup. Use electrolytes.

Rest days: creatine with any meal. Protein shake only if needed.
