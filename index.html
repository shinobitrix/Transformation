Timing Guide

Lift days: 45 min before training: pre-workout + 5g creatine. Within 30 min after: protein shake.

Trip days: Eat every 3–4 hours. Pack meals, shake, jerky/protein backup, and electrolytes.

Rest days: Creatine with any meal. Protein shake only if needed.
