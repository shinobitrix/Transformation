10-Year Progression Roadmap

Years 1–2: Foundation. Full-body strength, form, fat loss, steady progression.

Years 3–4: Intermediate muscle-building. More dumbbell/barbell variations and volume.

Years 5–6: Strength + hypertrophy blend. Heavier top sets and accessory work.

Years 7–8: Advanced variety. More unilateral, tempo, and specialization blocks.

Years 9–10: Longevity performance. Joint-friendly strength, conditioning, and muscle maintenance.

The app automatically rotates exercises by 8-week blocks and deloads every 4th week. You can still edit any exercise, sets, reps, or weight.
